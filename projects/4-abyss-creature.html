Abyss Creature

This is an originally designed water creature, marking my initial venture into organic design and the first monster I have ever crafted. This project was a significant learning experience for me, teaching me valuable skills in UV tiling, retopology, rigging, and animation using various softwares like Marmoset, Blender, Substance Painter, and Nomad.
